========================
    Spain
========================

Bus:
    -  Movelia - https://www.movelia.es
        -   A bus meta search engine. Might be best option.
    -  Estacion autobuses Madrid - http://www.estacionautobusesmadrid.com/
        -   No arrival times! So can't determine journey length.
        -   Need to click through to the actual bus provider in order to get details.
        -   But then why not just use the bus providers? Ah, too many of them. 
        -   Click on "Enlaces de Interes" on the left for a list of providers. A lot!
    -   Eurolines - http://www.eurolines.es/en/
        -   Indexed from Estacion autobuses Madrid.
        -   International only.
    -   AISA - http://www.aisa-grupo.com/
        -   No web interface, PDF schedule
    -   ALSA - http://www.alsa.es/
    -   Avanza Bus - http://www.avanzabus.com/web/default.aspx

Train:
    -   RENFE
        -   http://www.renfe.com/EN/viajeros/horarios.html
        -   Mobile site: http://renfe.mobi, click on Timetable
    -   FEVE
        -   http://www.feve.es/es/

References:
    -   Bus travel in Spain: http://gospain.about.com/od/bustravelinspain/Bus_Travel_in_Spain.htm
